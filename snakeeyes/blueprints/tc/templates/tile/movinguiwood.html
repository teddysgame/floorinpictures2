Movingui Wood tiles are one of the heavy embrossed pattern tile you can find in the vinyl collection. This is a good substitute of hardwood or parquet flooring. Consider to use this colour tile if:

  • You want an somewhere between a blend of moderate embrossed and moderate pattern environment.
  • You have large rooms or hallways....
  • You want the feel of solidness.
  • You have beige or light brown coloured furniture.
  • You want eye catching floorings.

Backstory & Origins

Movingui wood also known as the Nigerian Satinwood or Ayan wood is famous for their gorgeous deep yellow golden satin colours that glows with a tiger-eye effect.
Due to its glowing properties, it is commonly used as jewelry boxes, veneer, and high class furniture. This emerging wood is a Leguminous Tree found in western Africa and is high prized.
