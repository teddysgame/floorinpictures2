Interior Advice

Expert Review: Mr. Tee - Interior Consultant
4/5

Plum Dosato is pretty similar to Hazel Plum in most way. This is a good substitute of light coloured hardwood or parquet flooring. Wood or parquet's origins came from 17th century France and is only used among the wealthiest frenchman and royalties. If you are into exotic looks, and looking for a better manageable,durable flooring, Plum Dosato is perfect for you.


Consider to use this colour tile if:

  • You want an old ambience environment.
  • You have large rooms or hallways....
  • You want the feel of elegance.
  • You want eye catching floorings.
