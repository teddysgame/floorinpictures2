Interior Advice

Expert Review: Mr. Tee - Interior Consultant
4/5

Macacauba wood is the most comfortable to step on among all tiles in this series. This is because it has a thick 5mm soft vinyl and minimal woodgrain surface allowed it to be comfortable. Another advantage of a thick vinyl tile is it will not cool as quicky as ceramic tiles which will be uncomfortable to some at night or in an air conditioned environment. A warmer tile is espeically good for younger children or elderly people to walk on which is cares for their feet and allow for better anti-slip. This kind of floorings are commonly used in kitchens to compliment dark coloured kitchen cabinets. Some also laid it in living rooms for a direct alternative to parquet. Using this colour vinyl hides flooring imperfections and stains while maintaining its darker attributes....


Consider to use this colour tile if:

  • A warmer tile that does not chill the feet.
  • If you are in an air conditioned or cold environment.
  • You want a comfortable tile to walk on.
  • You have young infants or children in your environment.
  • You have elderly people in your environment.
  • You need better anti-slip functions.
  • Its for your kitchen.
  • You have dark coloured kitchen cabinets.
  • Its for your living room.
  • You have bright coloured walls.
