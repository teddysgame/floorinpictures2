Backstory & Origins

The word Ambrosia is an ancient Greek word of drinks and food for the gods that depicts immortality and longevity upon consumption. In the myth, Ambrosia is flown in to Olympus by doves to the Greek gods. Cedar however is a genus coniferous tree in the family of Pinaceae. Cedar trees are often found in the mountains of the Himalayas and Mediterranean region.

Cedar are popular ornament trees. Cedar wood and oil are also known to be natural repellent for moths. Due to its properties, cedar are used to make furnitures or chest that stores woollens that moths are attracted to.

Fun fact: Cedar trees are also used as training tree for bonsai cultivation.
