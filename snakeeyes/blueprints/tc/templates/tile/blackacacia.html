Interior Advice

Expert Review: Mr. Tee - Interior Consultant
4/5

Black Acacia wood can be found in Australia with a few other species found in asia and it is the lightest coloured tile among the Dark Wood series. A personal favourite for western theme interior design. Dark colours are generally a luxury symbol due to its capability to make its surrounding space seem smaller. Therefore it is installed in larger areas. Dark colours are more in trend and growing with popularity. It is the more preferable to men. It is the perfect darkest colour among the Dark Wood series. It gives a solid timeless effect to your floor. This coloured tile matches well with white or light coloured furnitures or contemporary interior design.


Consider to use this colour tile if:

  • You have a larger floor space. Dark coloured tiles tend to make the surroundings look smaller.
  • You have less activity in the space....
  • You can carefully maintain the floor from scratches. Scratches will be more visible because the wood underneath are normally of a much lighter colour.
  • You have light coloured cabinets or furnitures. The larger the contrast the better it looks visually.
  • You are able to maintain your floor more frequently. Darker coloured tiles requires more attention and more frequent cleaning.
  • Also consider whether are you living in here only for the short term. Lighter coloured tiles is a better preference to buyers.
  • You have a light colour wall. If your wall colour is already dark coloured. Darker colours already make the space look smaller, so you may want to contrast it with a light coloured floor to prevent the space from shrinking even further.
