Interior Advice

Expert Review: Mr. Tee - Interior Consultant
4/5

Black Palm is the lightest dark coloured tile which has a better pattern in-grain. Most people use this in kitchens and bathrooms to mask stain and dirty. Use this tile as well if you have a heavier traffic in the area. However scratches will be more apparent so do take precautionary steps in securing sharp objects such as furnitures to not puncture the flooring surface. This is an alternative tile to the Dark Chocolate series.


Consider to use this colour tile if:

  • Its for your kitchen.
  • Its for your living room.
  • You have moderate traffic in your area....
  • You have dark coloured kitchen cabinets.
  • You have bright coloured walls.
