The biggest difference between WPC vinyl tiles and luxury vinyl tiles are the thickness and the way it is being manufactured. WPC stands for Wood Plastic Composite. Both of these tiles are made from vinyl materials. WPC tiles however are much thicker and is coated with a water proofing layer. WPC are generally more expensive per sqft.
WPC however are not commonly used as a preferred tile installation because cost wise and benefits are not compelling.
We do not carry WPC tiles at the moment. We may carry some of it in the future if there are enough demand.
