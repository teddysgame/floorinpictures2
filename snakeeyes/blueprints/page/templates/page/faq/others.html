Depending on the pattern on your tiles, it may be quite obvious. However do not confuse grout lines with gappings. Gappings are a common problem especially towards thinner tiles.

Do ask yourself and have answers for all this before you undertake this installation. It will save a you a ton of headaches. Do speak to your installer regarding this as well.

Vinyl floorings require an even surface to prevent problems from surfacing in the future. Do not close an eye to this.

You would not want your door to scratch your floor in case it expands. It will also cost you money to trim the door. So ensure you have the right thickness of your vinyl flooring.

Does your door open outwards or inwards into the room?

You are not allowed to trim a fire door. You need to choose a vinyl tile thickness to accomodate your fire door.

Does it suit your style? Lighter colours looks great at the beginning but they are more prone to stain after some time.

The more the traffic, the easier it is to stain. The tile gaps will then darken which is hard to clean.

Mopping will make gap lines darker and ruin a perfect white floor with gap lines.
