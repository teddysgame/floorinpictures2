Market rate is about RM1 to RM2 for foreign labour hacking. We charge somewhere from RM2 to 3 depending on how big the floor size is. The reason we are charging above market rate is because our team possess the following criteria:

  • We are all local Malaysians installer.
  • We have strict standards of procedures to hack.
  • We take into consideration into protecting your property from damaging the unnecessary.
  • We have strict procurements to dispose of the waste
  • We will send e-reports to you about the progress at 10-50-100 percent together with all known issues during your hacking process.

  • Water and electricity cost are uncounted for although not substantial since most hacking or installation works are done within 1 or 2 days.
  • Uneven surface patching cost which are mostly necessary for a smooth surface vinyl flooring.
  • Door trimming cost in case doors are too low to be safe to close and open. Door trimming cost about RM50 to RM80 depending on the type of door.
  • Skirtings and glues are often underestimated.
  • Fire doors will not be able to trim as it is against regulations. Do take into consideration.
